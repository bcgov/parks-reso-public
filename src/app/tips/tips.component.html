Keys to visiting the great outdoors

1

Plan ahead

Prepare for your trip. Travel within your abilities, and check weather conditions before your trip. Have your park pass printed or saved before you arrive at the park, as mobile internet connectivity can be unreliable. Share your plan with a trusted contact.

2

Use available resources

Check the BC Parks home page for important updates and notices. Download and use the AdventureSmart Trip Plan app.

3

Pack the essentials

Nature is unpredictable. Do your research and pack the essentials. Be prepared for anything.

4

Recreate responsibly

Parks are not self-cleaning. Pack out all garbage, recyclables, and organics. Organic matter can attract wildlife. Learn more on our responsible recreation page.

5

Keep wildlife wild

Parks are home to all kinds of wildlife. Follow in-park signage to respect their natural habitat, and limit encounters. Please do not feed any wild animals.

6

Enjoy

Enjoy your visit. For more information on the day pass pilot program, including what to expect in the park, please visit the day-use pass information page.
