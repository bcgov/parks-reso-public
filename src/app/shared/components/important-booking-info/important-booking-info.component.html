Book passes starting at 7:00am, two days before your visit.

  • There are a set number of passes available each day. Cancelled passes become available again, so check back frequently.
  • You may reserve one of each pass type (AM, PM, and ALL DAY) per day.
  • You cannot transfer your pass to another date, time, location, or person.
  • Once you select an available pass, you have 7 minutes to complete your booking. After 7 minutes, the pass will be made available to others.
