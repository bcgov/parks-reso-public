Safety
Be prepared. Get to know the AdventureSmart's 3Ts.
Check the park page for advisories.
Create a trip plan.
Be Bear Aware. Avoid or stow attractants to help avoid encounters.
Stay on the trail. Shortcutting endagers other visitors with rolling debris.
Pack sufficient watter or be prepared to treat fresh water. There are no significant water sources before the 7 km mark on the trail.
BC Parks strongly advises against summiting Black Tusk.
Visitor guidelines
Respect other park users. Do not play music out loud.
There are no garbage cans along the trail. Understand your responsibility to #LeaveNoTrace by packing out anything you pack in.
Pets are not permitted in Garibaldi Park.
